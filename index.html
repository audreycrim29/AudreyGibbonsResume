I am a fifth year student at UCR, currenlty wrapping up my degree in Art History. I was first drawn to this feild in highschool after taking AP Art History. It felt natural because I have always loved history, and I have always been an artist ever since I was a child.

I currently work as a barista at a major coffee corporation. It is my second job and I have been with this company for almost three years now, I quite enjoy making coffee and getting to know my regular customers. It has been a great job to get me through school, especially since I can transfer anywhere, and I have had to considering that I have moved three times in the past three years. I am very content as a barista for now but hope to someday expand my career into the feild of Art History.

I am originally from Illinois but I have lived the majority of my life in California, and I intend to stay here as long as I can. When I'm not serving coffee, writing papers, or strolling through art museums, I like to spend time with my friends or create my own art. I mainly use watercolor or guache as my medium but I am also prolific in graphite, acrylic, and colored pencil.
